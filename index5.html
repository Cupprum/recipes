Spicy
Ramen

THE GOODS
1 Pack Ramen
1 Soft Egg
Chili Oil
Scallions
Nori
THE METHOD
  1. Boil the noodles for exactly 3 minutes until al dente.
  2. Whisk the flavor base with 2 tbsp of the starchy pasta water.
  3. Combine noodles and broth, then top with a swirl of chili oil.
  4. Garnish with a halved egg and fresh scallions.
