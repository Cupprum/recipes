# Chocolate Cookies

## Ingredients

* Melted butter - 115g
* Cane sugar - 150g
* Granulated sugar - 115g
* Egg - 1
* Vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon
* All-purpose flour - 160g
* Cocoa powder - 20g
* Baking powder - 1 teaspoon
* Salt - a pinch
* Chocolate chips - at least 100g (or to taste)

## Steps

1. Mix the melted butter with the cane sugar and granulated sugar
2. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the mixture
3. Sift the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt through a sieve into the mixture
4. Stir in the chocolate chips
5. Place the dough in the refrigerator for 20 minutes
6. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
7. Bake for 8–10 minutes at 160°C using the fan setting (convection)
