# Vegan Flan Pâtissier (Land&Monkeys Style)

## Ingredients

* Flour - 200g
* Vegan block butter - 100g (for crust) + 100g (for filling)
* Sugar - 25g (for crust) + 170g (for filling)
* Salt - 1 pinch
* Unsweetened soy milk - 1 Liter
* Cornstarch - 45g
* Agar-agar powder - 2 teaspoons
* Vanilla sugar - 2 tablespoons
* Turmeric - 1 teaspoon

## Steps

1. Mix flour, sugar, salt, and butter
2. Add water bit by bit, until a dough forms
3. Move the dough to the cake form, spread it out and put it into a freezer
4. In a small bowl, whisk the cornstarch, agar-agar, and 1/2 cup of the cold soy milk until smooth
5. In a large pot, combine the remaining soy milk, sugar, vanilla, and turmeric and warm it up so its almost boiling
6. Whisk the cornstarch/agar mixture into the hot milk and whisk constantly for 2–3 minutes until thick
7. Remove from heat and stir in the vegan butter until its completely melted
8. Pour the custard into the chilled pastry shell
9. Bake at 180°C for 50–60 minutes until the top has dark brown patches
10. Let the flan reach room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 6 hours
