# Vegan Flan Pâtissier (Land&Monkeys Style)

## Ingredients

* Flour - 200g
* Vegan block butter (cold and cubed) - 100g (for crust) + 100g (for filling)
* Caster sugar - 25g (for crust) + 170g (for filling)
* Ice-cold water - 2–3 tbsp
* Salt - 1 pinch
* Unsweetened soy milk - 1 Liter
* Cornstarch - 45g
* Agar-agar powder - 1.5 tbsp
* Vanilla paste or seeds - 2 tbsp (or 2 pods)
* Turmeric - 1 tiny pinch

## Steps

1. Mix flour, sugar, salt, and cold butter until crumbly
2. Add water 1 tablespoon at a time until a dough forms
3. Roll the dough out, line a deep 8-inch (20cm) pan, and chill in the freezer
4. In a small bowl, whisk the cornstarch, agar-agar, and 1/2 cup of the cold soy milk until smooth
5. In a large pot, combine the remaining soy milk, sugar, vanilla, and turmeric and bring to a gentle simmer
6. Whisk the cornstarch/agar mixture into the hot milk and whisk constantly for 2–3 minutes until thick
7. Remove from heat and stir in the vegan butter until melted and glossy
8. Pour the custard into the chilled pastry shell
9. Bake at 180°C for 50–60 minutes until the top has dark brown scorched patches
10. Let the flan reach room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 6 hours or overnight to set
