# Nutella Soufflé

## Ingredients

* Nutella - 100g
* Flour - 3 tablespoons
* Cocoa powder - 1 tablespoon
* Baking powder - 2/3 teaspoon
* Milk - 45 ml (or until the consistency is normal)

## Steps

1. Place the Nutella in a bowl
2. Add the flour, cocoa powder, and baking powder to the Nutella
3. Gradually pour in the milk while mixing
4. Continue adding milk and stirring until the mixture reaches a smooth, normal consistency
5. Pour the batter into bowls
6. Bake until the outside is set but the center remains slightly soft and risen
