# Zucchini Spread (Cukinová pomazanka)

## Ingredients

* Zucchini - cleaned and coarsely grated
* Onion
* Green or red bell pepper - finely chopped
* Tomato - finely chopped
* Processed cheese (Syr v črievku/karicka)
* Salt
* Black pepper
* Vegeta (seasoning salt)
* Wok seasoning
* Oil

## Steps

1. Salt the grated zucchini and let it sit to release its water
2. Finely chop the onion and sauté it in a pan with oil
3. Squeeze the excess water out of the zucchini
4. Add the zucchini and the finely chopped bell pepper to the pan
5. Sauté the mixture until it is nicely roasted
6. Season with salt, black pepper, vegeta, and wok seasoning
7. Stir in the processed cheese until combined
8. Finally, add the finely chopped fresh tomato and mix well
