# Vegan Mafé

## Ingredients

* Oil
* Onion - 1 medium
* Garlic - 3 cloves
* Tomato paste - 15 ml
* Chopped tomatoes - 265g (2/3 of a 400g can)
* Ginger - 3 cm piece
* Carrots - 5 medium
* Kohlrabi (Kalerab) - 1 medium
* Celery - 4 sticks
* Curry paste - 1 tea spoon
* Sweet potato - 1 medium
* White beans - 1 can (approx. 400g)
* Peanut butter - 3-4 table spoons
* Cilantro
* Cumin
* Vegetable bouillon - 500 ml
* Rice

## Steps

1. Chop all the vegetables into bite-sized pieces
2. In a large pan, fry the chopped onion and ginger in oil until softened
3. Add the cumin and curry paste to the pan and stir for a minute
4. Add all the vegetables except for the white beans into the pan
5. Fry the vegetables briefly, then add the vegetable bouillon
6. Sauté for approximately 15 minutes until the vegetables are tender
7. Stir in the white beans, cilantro, and peanut butter
8. Cook for an additional 5 minutes and serve with rice
