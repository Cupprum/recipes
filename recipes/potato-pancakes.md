# Potato Pancakes (Zemiakove placky)

## Ingredients

* Potatoes - 1 kg
* Flour - 4-5 tablespoons
* Egg - 1
* Onion - 1 large
* Garlic - approx. 6 cloves (or garlic powder/paste)
* Ground black pepper - approx. 1 teaspoon
* Dried marjoram - 3-4 teaspoons
* Salt - 1 teaspoon
* Milk (or water) - 100-200 ml

## Steps

1. Coarsely grate the potatoes; if they are clean, just rinse thoroughly without peeling
2. Grate the onion or chop it finely and add to the potatoes
3. Finely grate the garlic and add to the mixture
4. Add the flour, egg, black pepper, marjoram, salt, and milk to a large bowl
5. Mix all ingredients together thoroughly
6. Heat a generous amount of oil in a pan for extra crispiness
7. Use a large spoon to place the mixture into the pan and form small pancakes
8. Fry at a medium-high heat until golden, ensuring they do not burn
