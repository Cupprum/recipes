# Caramel Crêpe Sauce

## Ingredients

* Sugar - 200g
* Crème fraîche - 200g
* Salted butter - 80-100g

## Steps

1. Melt the sugar alone in a pot
2. In a separate pot, heat the crème fraîche to reduce the thermal gradient
3. Once the sugar has turned into caramel, add the hot crème fraîche step by step while being careful of the steam
4. When the crème fraîche is fully incorporated, turn off the heat
5. Add the salted butter
6. Mix everything until the butter has completely melted
