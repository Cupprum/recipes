# Four Pizzas

## Ingredients

* Flour 00 - 600g
* Water - 360 ml
* Sugar - a pinch
* Salt - a pinch
* Yeast - half a pouch
* Olive oil

## Steps

1. Warm up water so its lukewarm
2. Move a bit of water into a bowl and mix it with salt
3. Add the yeast into the rest of water and mix
4. Let the yeast work for few minutes, so its bubbly
5. Mix everything in the bowl with salty water
6. Show the dough who is the boss
7. Spread a bit of olive oil all over the dough
8. Let the dough raise for around 12 hours
9. Move it to the fridge afterwards - it can stay there for up to around 4 days
10. Cut into 4 parts
11. Try to use the slap technique to make it into a pizza pie
12. Add marinara with some toppings
13. Spread a bit of olive oil on the edges
14. Heat up the oven to the highest possible temperature
15. Bake the pizza on the pizza stone for 6 minutes
