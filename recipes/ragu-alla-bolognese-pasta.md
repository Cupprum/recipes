# Ragú alla Bolognese Pasta

## Ingredients

* Beef (can be mixed with pork)
* Onions
* Celery
* Carrots
* Olive oil
* Wine (white or red)
* Tomato sauce
* Salt and pepper
* Rosemary (optional)
* Pasta (Penne)
* Recommended Brands: De Cecco, Rummo, Voiello

## Steps

1. Finely chop the onions, celery, and carrots into tiny pieces
2. Pour olive oil into a pot and heat for 2 minutes
3. Add the onions and cook on medium-low heat (setting 4-5) for 3–4 minutes until golden
4. Add the majority of the chopped vegetables and cook for up to 6 minutes
5. Add the meat and increase the heat (setting 7)
6. Stir the meat for about 5 minutes until it is browned
7. Pour in a little bit of wine and cook for 5 minutes
8. Add the tomato sauce and stir well
9. Reduce the heat to low (setting 4)
10. Cover the pot, leaving a small 2 cm gap
11. Simmer for at least one hour
12. Check and stir every 20 minutes, adding a bit more vegetables each time
13. Season with salt, pepper, and rosemary to taste
14. Boil the pasta according to the brand's instructions and serve with the sauce
