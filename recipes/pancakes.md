# Pancakes

## Ingredients

* Eggs - 4
* Flour - 200g
* Milk - 250 ml
* Sugar - 50g
* Baking powder - just a bit
* Lemon - a bit

## Steps

1. Separate whites from yolks
2. Mix whites with a bit of citron
3. Add 25g of sugar to whites
4. Make snow out of whites
5. Make dough out of yolks, flour, milk, rest of sugar and some baking powder
6. Mix in the snow by using spatula
