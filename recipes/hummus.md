# Hummus

## Ingredients

* Chickpeas - 2 packs (460g after draining)
* Tahini
* Lemon
* Cumin
* Olive oil
* Garlic - 1 clove

## Steps

1. Wash the chickpeas thoroughly until the water is no longer soapy
2. Boil the chickpeas for 20 minutes
3. Place the chickpeas in a blender
4. Add the remaining ingredients according to your preference and blend until smooth
