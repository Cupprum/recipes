# Spring Rolls

## Ingredients

* Rice spring roll paper
* Rice noodles
* Pak choi
* Bell pepper
* Spring onion
* Carrot
* Baby corn (or other vegetables)
* Protein (tofu, shrimp, or other)
* Soy sauce
* Peanut butter
* Garlic - 1 clove
* Lemon juice
* Chili

## Steps

1. Prepare the rice noodles according to packaging and mix with a bit of soy sauce
2. Chop the fresh vegetables (bell pepper, spring onion, carrot) and mix in a bowl
3. Chop the vegetables intended for frying (pak choi, baby corn, etc.)
4. Fry the vegetables with a bit of soy sauce
5. Fry the chosen protein in a pan until cooked
6. Mash the garlic into a bowl for the sauce
7. Add peanut butter, soy sauce, lemon juice, and a bit of chili to the garlic
8. Add water step by step and mix until the sauce reaches a creamy consistency
9. Adjust the sauce flavor by adding ingredients to taste
10. Soak the rice spring roll paper in a plate of water until flexible
11. Place the paper on a cutting board and straighten it out
12. Layer the noodles, fresh vegetables, fried vegetables, and protein onto the paper
13. Fold the sides of the paper inward first, then roll tightly to close
