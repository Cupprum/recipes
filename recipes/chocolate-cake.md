# Chocolate Cake (Brownie Style)

## Ingredients

* Flour - 1 cup
* Granulated sugar - 1 cup
* Butter - 2/3 cup
* Water - 1/2 cup
* Cocoa powder - 2 tablespoons
* Egg - 1
* Baking soda - 1/2 teaspoon
* Milk + Vinegar mixture - 1/4 cup (let sit)
* Vanilla extract - 1/2 teaspoon
* Butter - 1/3 cup
* Cocoa powder - 2 tablespoons
* Milk - 3 tablespoons
* Vanilla extract - 1/2 teaspoon
* Powdered sugar - 3 tablespoons
* Chocolate - to taste

## Steps

1. Grease a baking sheet
2. In a large bowl, mix together the flour and sugar
3. In a pot, combine the butter, water, and cocoa; stir until the mixture begins to bubble
4. Pour the hot pot mixture into the flour and sugar bowl and mix
5. In a separate small bowl, beat the egg, then mix it with the baking soda, soured milk (milk + vinegar), and vanilla extract
6. Combine all the components together into one smooth batter
7. Pour onto the baking sheet and bake at 200°C for 20 minutes
8. For the frosting, combine the butter, cocoa, and milk in a pot over heat
9. Once melted, stir in the powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and chocolate until smooth
10. Pour the frosting over the cake while still warm
