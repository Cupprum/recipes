# Jackfruit curry

## Ingredients
* Canned jackfruit - 2 250g cans = 500g
* Broccoli
* Green curry paste - 1.5 tea spoons
* Curry powder
* Onion - 1 medium
* Chopped tomatoes - 1 can
* Coconut milk - 1 can
* Salt
* Oil
* Rice

## Steps
1. Prepare the rice
2. Boil broccoli for 3 minutes
3. Cool the broccoli in cold water for a minute
4. Try to remove water from the broccoli
5. In a large pan stir fry chopped onions with jackfruit and green curry paste
6. Add chopped tomatoes and coconut milk into the pan
7. Boil for 15 to 20 minutes
