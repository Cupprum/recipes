# Cheesecake

## Ingredients

* Philadelphia type cream cheese - 500g
* Eggs - 4 (for filling) + 1 egg yolk (for base)
* Sugar - 200g
* Cooking cream - 250ml
* Flour - 1 tablespoon
* Cookies
* Water - a bit

## Steps

1. Crush the cookies for the base
2. Add one egg yolk with a bit of water to the crushed cookies and mix
3. Create the base by mashing the mixture into the pan as firmly as possible
4. Mix the cream cheese, 4 eggs, sugar, cooking cream, and flour together until smooth
5. Pour the filling over the cookie base
6. Preheat the oven to 200°C
7. Bake for 40 minutes
