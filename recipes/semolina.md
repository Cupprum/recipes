# Semolina Porridge (Krupicová kaša)

## Ingredients

* Semolina (Krupica)
* Milk
* Cinnamon
* Instant cocoa
* Sugar
* Salt
* Butter

## Steps

1. Measure one spoon of semolina for every 1 dl of milk
2. Add a pinch of sugar and a very small bit of salt into the milk
3. Heat up the milk
4. Add the semolina bit by bit in a steady, flowing stream
5. Note that the porridge will thicken further once it cools down
6. Sprinkle cinnamon and instant cocoa on top
7. Add a small piece of butter and a final pinch of salt to finish
