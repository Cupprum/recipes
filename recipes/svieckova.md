# Sviečková

## Ingredients
* Pork sirloin (or tempeh for a vegan option)
* Carrots
* Parsley root
* Celery root
* Bay leaves
* Rosemary
* Black pepper
* Cooking cream
* Water

## Steps
1. Brown the pork on all sides in a pan and season with rosemary, bay leaves, and black pepper.
2. Transfer the browned pork into a casserole dish.
3. Cook in the oven at 180°C for 50 minutes.
4. Clean and peel the carrots, parsley root, and celery root.
5. Cut the cleaned vegetables into small pieces.
6. Fry the vegetables in a pot with a bay leaf for approximately 15 minutes.
7. Add a small amount of water to the pot.
8. Remove all bay leaves from the mixture.
9. Blend the vegetable mixture in the pot until smooth, gradually adding water and cooking cream until the desired consistency is achieved.
