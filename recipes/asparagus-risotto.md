# Asparagus Risotto

## Ingredients

* Risotto rice - 250g
* Asparagus - 500g
* Water - a large amount
* Onion - 1 medium
* Bouillon - 1 cube
* Garlic - 2 large cloves
* Wine (for finishing)
* Oil

## Steps

1. Prepare bouillon by dissolving the cube in water
2. Sauté chopped onions in a pot, then add the risotto rice
3. Cook the rice by adding bouillon water gradually, stirring constantly to prevent burning
4. Once the rice is nearly cooked, add a splash of wine
5. Cut the asparagus into small pieces (2–3 cm long)
6. Sauté the asparagus in oil in a separate pan until soft
7. Add minced garlic and a small amount of bouillon water to the asparagus
8. Mix the cooked asparagus and its sauce into the rice
