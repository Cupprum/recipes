# Marinated Steak with Beans and Bacon

## Ingredients

* Prepackaged, marinated steak
* Coconut oil
* Butter
* Rosemary
* Salt
* Pepper
* Green beans
* Bacon
* Sunflower oil

## Steps

1. Roast the bacon in sunflower oil for 5 minutes
2. Add the green beans to the bacon and increase the heat
3. Add a bit more sunflower oil and salt to the bean mixture
4. Heat the coconut oil in a separate pan until it is very hot
5. Place the steak onto the hot pan
6. Season the steak with salt and pepper
7. Fry for 3 minutes on each side
8. Add butter and rosemary to the pan while cooking the second side
