# Pasta Aglio e Olio

## Ingredients

- 400g spaghetti
- 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 100ml extra virgin olive oil
- 1 red chili, diced (or chili flakes to taste)
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

## Steps

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
2. While the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil in a large pan over low heat.
3. Add the sliced garlic and chili to the oil. Cook gently for 2-3 minutes until the garlic is fragrant and light golden (don't brown it).
4. Reserve 200ml of pasta water, then drain the cooked pasta.
5. Add the drained pasta to the garlic-oil mixture and toss gently. Add some pasta water a little at a time to help create a light sauce.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Serve immediately, optionally garnished with fresh parsley.
