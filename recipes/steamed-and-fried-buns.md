# Steamed and Fried Buns (Parene Buchty)

## Ingredients

* Milk - 370g (range: 350g–390g)
* Flour - 500g
* Crystal sugar - 40g
* Yeast - 7g
* Strawberry jam
* Sunflower oil
* Water - 500 ml

## Steps

1. Warm the milk to room temperature in a pot
2. Mix the flour, sugar, and yeast in a bowl
3. Add the milk to the mixture step by step and mix well (kneading is not necessary)
4. Let the dough rise for 1 hour
5. Mash the dough into a ball and add a bit of flour to remove stickiness
6. Split the dough into 80g chunks
7. Roll each chunk out to 1cm thickness
8. Fill with jam and shape into balls
9. Let the balls rise for 15 minutes
10. Warm up a casserole pot and add a bit of oil
11. Fry the buns in the covered pot for 1 minute
12. Add half a liter of water and cover the pot again
13. Steam for 5 minutes
