# Caramelized Chicken (Karamelizované kura)

## Ingredients

* Chicken breast
* Salt
* Black pepper
* Orange juice
* Soy sauce
* Sugar
* Oil

## Steps

1. Cut the chicken breast into very small pieces, approximately 2–3 cm
2. Heat a pan with oil
3. Place the chicken pieces into the hot pan and stir
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Let the chicken simmer somewhat aggressively, stirring occasionally, until all the water from the meat has evaporated
6. Pour orange juice over the chicken pieces and stir
7. Add soy sauce, using about 1/3 of the amount of orange juice (or slightly less), and stir
8. Add a generous amount of sugar
9. Let the mixture bubble thoroughly until the sauce thickens and caramelizes
