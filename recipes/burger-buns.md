# Burger Buns

## Ingredients

* Flour - 400g (+ approx. 100g extra)
* Milk - 250 ml
* Water - 100 ml
* Yeast - 30g (fresh cube) or 8g (dry pack)
* Butter - 30g
* Sugar - 2 teaspoons
* Salt - 2 teaspoons
* Egg - 1

## Steps

1. Mix the milk, water, yeast, and butter together
2. In a separate bowl, mix the 400g of flour, salt, and sugar
3. Heat the liquid mixture to approximately 35°C (lukewarm/body temperature)
4. Add the egg and the warmed liquids to the dry ingredients
5. Mix the components together thoroughly
6. Gradually add the extra 100g of flour while mixing until the dough reaches the right consistency
