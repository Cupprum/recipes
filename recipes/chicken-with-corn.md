# Chicken with Corn (Kuracie s kukuricou aka Cina)

## Ingredients

* Chicken breast
* Garlic (fresh or dried)
* Salt
* Black pepper
* Canned corn
* Soy sauce
* Oil

## Steps

1. Cut the chicken breast into pieces roughly 2–3 cm in size
2. Place the chicken pieces into a bowl
3. Drizzle with oil and soy sauce
4. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic
5. Mix everything together thoroughly
6. Let the chicken marinate (ideally overnight, but at least 30 minutes)
7. Heat oil in a pan
8. Place the marinated chicken onto the hot pan
9. Stir occasionally until almost all the liquid has evaporated
10. Drain the canned corn and add it to the pan
11. Fry the mixture, stirring occasionally, until the corn is slightly browned and darker in color
