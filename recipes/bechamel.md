# Béchamel

## Ingredients

* Butter - 100g
* Flour - 100g
* Milk - 0.7 L (up to 1 L)
* Nutmeg - a generous amount

## Steps

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat
2. Add the flour and whisk constantly to create a roux
3. Gradually pour in the milk while whisking to avoid lumps
4. Continue to cook and stir until the sauce thickens to the desired consistency
5. Season with a generous amount of nutmeg
