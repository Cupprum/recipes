Dinner • 20 Mins

Honey Garlic Salmon

Ingredients

  • 2 Salmon Fillets
  • 3 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • Lemon Slices

The Process

01 Pat the salmon fillets dry and season with salt and pepper.
02 Whisk honey, soy sauce, and garlic in a small bowl to create the glaze.
03 Sear salmon in a hot pan for 3 minutes per side until golden.
04 Pour the glaze over the fish and simmer for 1 minute until thickened.
