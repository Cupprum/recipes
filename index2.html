Breakfast • 5 Mins

Cacio e Pepe

Ingredients

  • 200g Tonnarelli pasta
  • 1 cup Pecorino Romano
  • Fresh black pepper
  • Salt for water

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in lightly salted water.
  2. Toast crushed pepper in a dry pan.
  3. Mix cheese with pasta water to create a paste.
  4. Toss everything together until creamy.
