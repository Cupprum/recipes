Classic Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of sourdough bread
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • A pinch of sea salt
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Toast the bread until golden and crisp.
  2. Mash the avocado in a small bowl with a fork.
  3. Spread the avocado evenly across the toast.
  4. Season with salt and red pepper flakes.
