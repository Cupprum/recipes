Lemon Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • Pasta 200g
  • Garlic 4 Cloves
  • Olive Oil 1/4 Cup
  • Chili Flakes 1 tsp
  • Lemon Juice 1/2 Lemon
  • Parmesan To taste

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water. Cook pasta 1 minute less than package directions.
  2. While pasta cooks, thinly slice garlic. In a cold pan, add oil and garlic, then turn heat to medium.
  3. When garlic is golden, add chili flakes and a splash of pasta water to stop the cooking.
  4. Toss the pasta in the oil with lemon juice and parsley until glossy. Serve with cheese.
