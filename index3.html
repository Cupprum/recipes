Classic
Negroni

Ingredients

  • 30ml Gin
  • 30ml Campari
  • 30ml Sweet Vermouth
  • Orange peel garnish

Preparation

  1. 01 Stir all ingredients in a mixing glass with plenty of ice.
  2. 02 Strain into a rocks glass over a large clear ice cube.
  3. 03 Express the oils of an orange peel over the glass and drop it in.
